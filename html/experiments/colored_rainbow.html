Introduction

Colored Rainbow is one of the 12 currently available Experiments in Shifted in Monochrome, being one of three to represent the Rainbowverse.

Appearance

Colored Rainbow is a vibrant blue unicorn with a neon green mane and tail, both having bright purple stripes. Her horn is the same vibrant blue color as her body is. Her eyes are a light blue color. On her hind thighs is a mark shaped like a simplistic camera with a white triangle in the center.

Personality

Colored Rainbow is a helpful and careful pony, always willing to assist others in a time of difficulty or need. Whether it be for physical work, helping in planning, or assistance on an emotional level, Colored Rainbow makes sure she's a pony others can count on. She's a huge fan of photography, getting her cutie mark after realizing she had a near perfect knack for a good photo and seizing the moment then and there.

While she is often times quiet and keeps to herself, she can be overly energetic around people she is close with, but isn't afraid to stay in the background incase something more important is coming up.

In Shifted in Monochrome, she is a bit more energetic, influenced by another entity within the void.

Abilities

Shielded Safety

Keybind: E
Cooldown: TBD

Colored Rainbow, utilizing her unicorn magic, creates a forcefield around herself or another Experiment that she selects. The shield lasts for 60 seconds, or until hit by an Eradicator. The shield won't stun the Eradicator upon being hit, but applies an extra 0.5 seconds of endlag to their primary attack.

During "Last Creature Standing", the shield is able to negate 2 hits instead of just 1, with the second hit applying a bit of knockback to the Eradicator.

Speed-Up

Keybind: Q
Cooldown: TBD

Colored Rainbow assists another Experiment by boosting their speed for 3 seconds. Speed-Up cannot be used on Colored Rainbow herself.

During "Last Creature Standing", Colored Rainbow can use Speed-Up on herself, however it only lasts for 1.5 seconds instead of 3 seconds.

Teleport

Keybind: X
Cooldown: TBD

Colored Rainbow turns into a small ball of light, drastically increasing her speed for 2 seconds.

During "Last Creature Standing", Colored Rainbow turns fully invisible instead of turning into a small ball of light, however the ability only lasts for 1 second instead of 2.

Trivia

  • Colored Rainbow was originally made using random MLP bases Redux found on DeviantArt.
  • Colored Rainbow is not the same as GMod Colored Rainbow.
  • She has no sense of fight, mostly just flight unless she's cornered.
  • She often gets called "Colors" or "Rainbow" by other ponies, however requests not to be referred to by those nicknames if near Colors or RainbowRedux to avoid confusion.
  • She cannot count to 70.
    • She can however count from 90 to 100, just not from 70 to 90.
