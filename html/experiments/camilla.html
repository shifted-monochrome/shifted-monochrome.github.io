Abilities

Type Resistance & Blaze

Keybind: N/A (Passive Abilities)
Cooldown: N/A (Passive Abilities)

Type Resistance: Camilla takes 50% less damage from Burn, is Frostbitten for 50% less time than other Experiments, but suffers a 50% speed reduction while Waterlogged. Additionally, she cannot use her second or fourth abilities while Waterlogged.

Trainer Bond: When near Dankles, Snow & Tempo's stuns will last for 50% longer. This ability is shared with Dankles.

Blaze: When at a third of her maximum health, Camilla's fire-based abilities become stronger. Fire Spin has a wider area of effect and lasts for longer, and Flamethrower stuns after a few seconds.

Psychic & Psybeam

Keybind: E
Cooldown: TBD

Camilla extends her stick, shooting out a psychic projectile that holds the Eradicator in place if they come into contact with it. While the Eradicator is grabbed, Camilla moves slower. If Psychic is used again while holding an Eradicator, Camilla will throw them away from her. The Eradicator can mash jump to break free faster or simply wait a few seconds.

During "Last Man Standing", Psychic becomes Psybeam. She can charge up and fire a psychic beam, that stuns the Eradicator when hitting them.

Fire Spin

Keybind: Q
Cooldown: TBD

Camilla launches a ball of fire out from her stick, creating a medium-sized ring of fire where it lands for 7.5 seconds. The ball is affected by gravity. Walking into the ring of fire inflicts Burn on both Eradicators and Experiments alike, and slows them down while they stand in it. Camilla herself is immune to the ring of fire.

Witch's Flight

Keybind: X
Cooldown: TBD

Camilla stops in place, using magic to enlargen her stick before climbing onto it and riding it. She can freely fly around for as long as her stamina meter lasts, and can cancel the ability early to shorten the cooldown. While flying, Camilla can grab up to two other Experiments and carry them with her, at the cost of reduced flight speed.

Eruption & Attract

Keybind: Z
Cooldown: TBD

Camilla creates a short-lasting spinning cylinder of flames around her, knocking the Eradicator away with a short stun time. This ability has a small chance to inflict Burn on the Eradicator. If used while standing next to Dankles, Camilla lights his fists on fire, allowing him to inflict Burn while using Punch.

During "Last Man Standing", Attract replaces Eruption. Camilla stops in place and teases the Eradicator, inflicting the Infatuated status depending on the Eradicator's sexuality. This ability only works while in close range to the Eradicator.
