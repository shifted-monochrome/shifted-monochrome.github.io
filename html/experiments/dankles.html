Introduction

Dankles is one of the 12 currently available Experiments in Shifted in Monochrome, being one of three to represent BriceTAS.

Appearance

Dankles looks nearly identical to Knuckles the Echidna from the Sonic the Hedgehog franchise, however wears pixelated sunglasses with a white shine.

Abilities

Glide & Climb, Fire & Ice, and Trainer Bond

Keybind: Space (Glide & Climb), N/A (Fire & Ice and Trainer Bond)
Cooldown: N/A (Passive Abilities)

Glide & Climb: Pressing jump twice allows Dankles to glide slowly. He can glide indefinitely, however falls faster the longer it's in use, with the fall speed only resetting upon touching the ground. Jumping or gliding into a wall makes Dankles cling to it, allowing him to then climb up or down it. He has a stamina bar for how long he can climb, and can glide out of it.

Fire & Ice: Snow is able to use Icy Wind to freeze Dankles' fists for a single punch combo, giving their punch a chance to apply Frostbite to an Eradicator. In a similar manner, Camilla can use Eruption on Dankles' fists to have him apply a Burn effect instead. These elemental effects apply to Grand Slam's shockwave as well.

Trainer Bond: When near Snow & Tempo, Dankles' stuns will last for 50% longer. This ability is shared with Snow & Tempo.

Punch

Keybind: E
Cooldown: TBD

Dankles lunges forward with a punch that stuns. He can use this 3 times at a time for a combo, extending the Eradicator's stun timer on each hit. It'll go on cooldown a few seconds after each use. (For example, if Dankles only does one punch, the move will go on cooldown after 2 seconds.)

Punch turns into Grand Slam while in the air or after jumping. Dankles punches into the ground after falling, causing a shockwave. Said shockwave gets stronger depending on how long Dankles fell for. Snow and Camilla's buffs still apply.

Block

Keybind: Q
Cooldown: TBD

Dankles stops in place, holding up his fists and blocking. The block lasts for 1 second. If Dankles is hit during this time, he'll negate damage dealth to him and jab the Eradicator, stunning them for a very short period of time.

Relic Boost

Keybind: X
Cooldown: TBD

This ability is only available during "Last Creature Standing". Dankles performs an uppercut that acts as both a stun option and either a high jump or a double jump depending on if he is grounded when using it. The attack can be charged while on the ground to send Dankles higher, or can be used in the air to send them upwards at a set height. Glide can be used after using this move.
