Introduction

Stitchfur is one of the 12 currently available Experiments in Shifted in Monochrome, being one of three to represent SomeDumbFurry.

Appearance

Sticthfur is a primarily black feline, with several segments of varying patterns stitched together, such as his left ear and front left leg. He has a heart stitched onto his chest with a striped orange pattern. He has heterochromia, with his left eye being blue and having a scar on it, and his right eye being green.

Personality

Based on his dialogue, Stitchfur is quite the cocky and confrontational individual.

Stitchfur's playstyle greatly depends on which soul he has. Some souls cater towards a more cautious or evasive playstyle, while others cater more towards a rusher-like one.

Abilities

Pain Aura

Keybind: N/A (Passive Ability)
Cooldown: N/A (Passive Ability)

When an Experiment is injured, they will have a small indicator that Stitchfur can see through walls.

Pounce

Keybind: E
Cooldown: TBD

Stitchfur crouches down, preparing to lunge and slash at the Eradicator. He moves much slower while crouching, but all Eradicators in a 10 stud radius are marked. Upon pressing M1, the Eradicator will be hit as long as they're within said radius, but not if the Eradicator is too close to Stitchfur. Pressing E again will cancel the ability.

Wild Charge

Keybind: Q
Cooldown: 35s

Stitchfur charges forwards, recklessly slashing at whatever is in his path. Due to its high strength, it can damage Experiments for 10 damage alongside stunning Eradicators for 5 seconds.

Meow

Keybind: X
Cooldown: TBD

Stitchfur meows, marking himself to everybody in a 10 stud radius.

Swap

Keybind: Z
Cooldown: TBD

Stitchfur's primary mechanic. Using Swap will switch out which of four souls follow and buff Stitchfur with another randomly selected soul. One random soul is granted at the beginning of each round, with the cooldown only being 5 seconds for the first use of Swap.

  • Silver Speed (Feline): Indicated by WIND, Silver Speed embodies agility and speed. Stitchfur benefits from increased speed and reduced cooldowns, but in return stuns for shorter time due to the soul lacking in strength. Pounce's radius and Wild Charge's duration are increased.
  • Wolfheart: Indicated by THUNDER, Wolfheart embodies stealth and patience. Stitchfur benefits from having increased damage resistance, a drastic increase to Pounce's radius, and can see the Eradicator at all times. His speed and stun time are reduced as a result of the soul's cautiousness.
  • Mistyfoot: Indicated by WATER, Mistyfoot embodies strength and awareness. Stitchfur benefits from having increased stun times and an inability to be marked by the Eradicator. His speed is reduced due to the soul's inability to properly fight on land.
  • Morningstar: Indicated by DARK, Morningstar embodies persistence and determination. Stitchfur benefits from a massive increase in stun time and a slight speed boost, but takes more damage. Wild Charge's cooldown is reduced slightly, with each use inflicting increasing damage on Stitchfur themselves, and Pounce's cooldown gets longer due to the soul's headstrongness and refusal to hide away.

During Stitchfur's last life, two of the souls at random are completely gone, leaving the other two to be the only obtainable souls.
