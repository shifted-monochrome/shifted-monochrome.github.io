Introduction

Gray is one of the 12 currently available Experiments in Shifted in Monochrome, being one of three to represent Inkblot. They are very close friends with Specter, and have a fairly complex history with Inkblot.

Appearance

Gray is an anthropomorphic feline-like creature with primarily gray fur, having light gray fur in their ears, on the tip of their tail, and around their paws and footpaws. Their paw beans are similarly light gray. They have a large censor bar covering their eyes and a stitched up mouth. They wear a medium gray hoodie with a darker gray hood, pockets, and white drawstrings. They also have dark gray pants and medium gray boots.

Personality

Gray tends to keep to themselves, preferring to avoid social interaction if possible. If they can't avoid it though, they'll often let Specter do most of the talking, as Gray feels comfortable talking to Specter. They can be a bit jumpy at times due to having fairly bad generalized anxiety.

In Shifted in Monochrome, their personality gives way to a shy yet helpful one. They're a bit more talkative, but barely so.

Abilities

Anxiety & Anomalous Physiology

Keybind: N/A (Passive Abilities)
Cooldown: N/A (Passive Abilities)

Anxiety: Gray slowly regenerates health at a rate of 1 health every 3 seconds. During "Last Man Standing", Anxiety gives way to Panic Attack, increasing the rate of health regeneration to 1 health every 1.5 seconds.

Anomalous Physiology: Gray takes extra damage from attacks that inflict Frostbite, extra damage from fire-based attacks, and is slowed for longer when frozen. They cannot enter underwater areas due to their bouyancy, and accumulate Frostbite 25% faster from Snow's Mist ability.
They receive 15% less damage from Redux.

Reap

Keybind: E
Cooldown: 15s

Gray swings their scythe in a wide but relatively slow horizontal arc, dealing high knockback with a stun time of 6 seconds.

Sow

Keybind: Q
Cooldown: 20-25s

Gray swings their scythe in a vertical arc, sending out a wave of ink that travels for 6-10 studs depending on how long it's charegd for. It has a windup time of at minimum 3 seconds, with each additional level of charge adding 1 second. Higher charge results in a thinner wave that travels farther, while lower charge results in a thicker wave that doesn't travel as far. Your speed is reduced while charging.

Mend

Keybind: X
Cooldown: 35s

Gray creates an ring of ink around them. Experiments within this aura will be healed for a maximum of 75 health over 15 seconds (5 health per second), with Gray being slowed by 50% during this time. Mend can be cancelled early by using the ability again.

Trivia

  • Gray's Anomalous Physiology passive ability is based on their canon inability to regulate their body heat following their interaction with Inkblot.
    • During colder months or colder weather, they'll reluctantly allow physical contact, but otherwise don't like it from most.
    • They'll also lay with Specter at night due to Specter being warm, and Specter liking having something to wrap their arms around while they sleep. Despite this, they insist they're just roommates.
  • They strongly dislike reflective surfaces, such as mirrors or polished metal, as they "don't see themselves staring back at them."
  • Gray doesn't require sleep or sustenance, however will sometimes indulge in the latter. How the sustenance is absorbed into their body is still unknown.
