Introduction

Snow & Tempo are one of the 12 currently available Experiments in Shifted in Monochrome, being one of three to represent BriceTAS.

Appearance

Snow & Tempo appear identical to a Glaceon and Meloetta's "Aria Forme".

Gameplay

(Hypothetical, no gameplay yet.)

On their first life, Snow & Tempo play well as support. Their abilities help both themselves, and other experiments. On her last life though, Snow becomes more of an offensive Experiment, gaining abilities that can be used to effectively stun or deter the Eradicator.

Abilities

Type Resistance, Trainer Bond, & Ice-Cold Fury

Keybind: N/A (Passive Abilities)
Cooldown: N/A (Passive Abilities)

Type Resistance: Snow is completely resistant to Frostbite, but takes more damage from Burn.

Trainer Bond: When near Dankles, Snow & Tempo's stuns will last for 50% longer. This ability is shared with Dankles.

Ice-Cold Fury: Upon reaching "Last Creature Standing", Snow's ice-based stuns will stun for longer.

Icy Wind & Ice Fang

Keybind: E
Cooldown: TBD

Snow shoots a ball of ice from her mouth, which travels in the direction of the cursor. Upon hitting an Eradicator, it has a chance to inflict Frostbite. This ability can freeze Dankles' punch.

On her last life, Icy Wind is replaced with Ice Fang. Snow lunges forwards and bites the Eradicator with large, icy fangs, stunning the Eradicator with a chance of inflicting Frostbite.

Mist

Keybind: Q
Cooldown: TBD

Snow stomps the ground and creates a large cloud of mist around her that lasts for a few seconds. Any Experiments in this mist get a free dodge, however remaining in it for too long inflicts Frostbite on them.

Relic Prayer & Roar

Keybind: X
Cooldown: TBD

Snow stops in place while Tempo sings. A ring of music notes forms around the duo, and any Experiments within this ring (Snow included) will slowly be healed. Snow is unable to move during this time, however the ability can be cancelled early.

On her last life, Relic Prayer is replaced with Roar. Snow stops in place and roars, knocking back the Eradicator. This ability does not stun.

Helping Hand & Retaliate

Keybind: Z
Cooldown: TBD

Tempo sings, buffing Snow and one other Experiment near the duo. The buff has a chance of granting either 50% increased movement speed, or 50% damage resistance for five seconds. Each buff has a unique color of music notes trailing behind those afflicted with them.

On her last life, Helping Hand is replaced with Retaliate. Snow stops in place and creates a large tornado of ice around her, dealing heavy knockback and inflicting Frostbite on the Eradicator. This ability can only be used for a short period of time after being attacked.
