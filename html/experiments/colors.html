Introduction

Colors is one of the 12 currently available Experiments in Shifted in Monochrome, being one of three to represent the Rainbowverse.

Appearance

Colors is a light-skinned human with quite the colorful appearance. Their hair is a bright magenta color, and they wear a slightly paler beanie. Their shirt is a green color, and their jacket is reddish-violet. They wear dark tan shoes and blue cargo pants.

Personality

Colors is a distant, somewhat cold introvert who only keeps himself in a few circles, mostly The Misfit Trio back in his home world, or The Redux Variants in the Creative Void's "Rainbode" Zone. He would rather lead a team and get something over with quickly than take orders from someone else, or lying down and simply helping others.

Abilities

Dodge

Keybind: N/A (Passive Ability)
Cooldown: N/A (Passive Ability)

Upon being hit by an Eradicator's main attack, Colors has a 10% chance to dodge it. The dodge would negate the attack's damage and send Colors directly behind the Eradicator, stunning them for 0.5 seconds.

Force of Wind & Staring Contest

Keybind: E
Cooldown: 6s (Force of Wind) / 3s (Staring Contest)

Colors uses his wind manipulation to create a small tornado where he is looking. The tornado will sweep up Eradicators and Experiments, with Eradicators being stunned for a brief period of time and being flung away from Colors. Experiments will be picked up by the tornado as well, but will be flung upwards instead of away.

During "Last Creature Standing", Force of Wind is replaced with Staring Contest. Upon using staring contest, Colors will remove his blindfold and glare at whatever is in front of him. If an Eradicator is within the projected cone, they will be stunned for 1.5 seconds.

Dash

Keybind: Q
Cooldown: 2s

Colors quickly dashes towards an Experiment or horizontally in the direction they're looking.

Manipulated Metal

Keybind: X
Cooldown: 8s

Colors uses his metal manipulation to create an array of metal spikes in front of him, rising from the ground. The spikes deal a large amount of stun, but little knockback.

Trivia

  • Due to a strange birth defect, prolonged eye contact with Colors can kill an individual. He wears a blindfold to prevent accidental deaths. His eyes also glow.
    • He is able to see through the blindfold.
  • Colors has anger issues that he masks with emotionless expressions and a distant personality.
  • Colors is bisexual.
  • Colors' iris colors are dependent on emotion or magic usage. When using magic, the iris on the same side as whichever hand is being most prominently used will change, while the other wont. As an example, if he's using his left hand, only his left iris will change colors.
    • When experiencing no extreme emotions and not using magic, his irises are white.
    • When using wind magic, his irises are green.
    • When using metal magic, his irises are purple.
    • When experiencing extreme rage, his irises are red.
  • Manipulated Metal works by pulling metallic minerals directly from the ground, forming them into spikes.
