Appearance

The Creative Void is a vast expanse of twisting white platforms, all surrounding a giant clock. The platforms wrap around in illogical ways, and the amount of platforms leads to there being several layers to drop down or climb. The Windows can be seen floating out of reach around the map, each one leading to another location in the Rainbowverse. Every so often, one of the Windows will flicker.
