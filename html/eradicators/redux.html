Introduction

Redux is one of the four currently available Eradicators in Shifted in Monochrome, representing the Rainbowverse.

Appearance

Redux is a human-like individual with fair skin, brown hair, fox ears, and a fox tail. Her eyes are a solid black color with white slits. She wears a dark gray crop top and a lighter gray full-zip jacket with a light blue hoodie. She additionally has a brown belt around her waist underneath the jacket. Some sections of her body have melted, with her legs being the most notable example.

Personality

Being a Fox Tifhish, Redux's personality is typically that of an hyperactive and curious individual, though with a sly and conniving side when it comes to pure strategy. Her usually friendly demeanor is thrown out of the window upon ingesting more than 5 grams of sugar of any kind, where she then enters a state of primal instinct referred to as a "Sugar Rush".

In Shifted in Monochrome, her personality is that of a sadistic and bloodthirsty killer. She taunts other Experiments in the majority of her voicelines, and is shown to be solely after the sugars found within blood. Despite this, those without sugar aren't spared from her wrath if they stand in her way.

Shapeshift

Keybind: X

Redux shapeshifts into one of the Experiments currently alive. While transformed, the radius in which her chase and terror radius music can be heard is reduced by 50%. Observant Experiments will be able to tell she has transformed, as her ears and tail remain regardless of who she has shifted into. Using any ability will revert her back to her normal form.

Keybind: Z
Cooldown: N/A

This ability can only be used while Redux has shapeshifted into another Experiment. Copycat is entirely dependent on which Experiment she has become, with the ability being identical to one of the Experiment's offensive abilities. This ability can only be used once per Shapeshift, after which she will revert to her normal form.

Shifted in Monochrome Lore

"14 dead, 22 still missing... Redux was on yet another one of her sugar rushes. After having accidentally swallowed a piece of chocolate that flew into her mouth upon shattering, she had already taken out and gathered enough blood to be covered in the substance, going for a total of 6 hours, her longest sugar rush yet...

Whilst roaming a forest looking for more blood, more sugar, she saw a familiar, but peculiar sight. A white, oval-shaped ring of flame-like magic was swirling in front of her, leading to what seemed to be an even denser forest. In her crazed state, she laughed to herself, then rushed in, propelling herself into the experimental realm, and sparing that timeline from the shapeshifting menace that was the Fox-Girl Tifhish..."

Trivia

  • Redux's melting body is a result of her not caring enough to maintain her form during her Sugar Rush, focusing solely on getting to her prey.
  • Sugar Rushes are caused by the chemical components of sugar blending with a Tifhish's infinitely-replicating mass.
  • Tifhishes are naturally born with animal-like attributes, but otherwise bear zero resemblance to any Earth creatures.
