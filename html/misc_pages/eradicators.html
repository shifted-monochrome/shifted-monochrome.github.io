Eradicators are the main threat in Shifted in Monochrome. Their goal is to kill every Experiment before the timer runs out. While the odds may be stacked against the Eradicator in terms of sheer numbers, strategic use of their abilities greatly increases their chances of victory.

Currently there are four available Eradicators, one representing each of the game's owners.
