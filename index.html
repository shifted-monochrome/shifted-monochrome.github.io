Welcome to the Shifted in Monochrome wiki!

Shifted in Monochrome is an asymmetrical horror game based upon OC content from its owners.
The goal of the game is to survive until the timer runs out as an Experiment, or to kill all of the Experiments within the time limit as an Eradicator.
